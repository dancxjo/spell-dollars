Spell Dollars

This is a demonstration of my programming skills. It is a response to this thread.

The programs take a dollar amount in the form $987.52, for example, and convert it to "nine hundred eighty-seven dollars and fifty-two cents." Incompatible entries produce an exception. The largest number that can be spelled out is -1 + one unvigintillion dollars and 99 cents (i.e. $1066 - 1¢).

Ultimately, I plan to execute this in several different languages, using several different approaches.
